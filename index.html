I am a PhD student in Physics at the Universidad de Chile, focused on the study of solar flares and their nonlinear dynamics using complex network tools.

My research combines observational data from space-based instruments (SDO, GOES) with theoretical frameworks from statistical mechanics and dynamical systems to understand the underlying mechanisms of magnetic energy release in the solar corona.

Advisor: Dr. Víctor Muñoz, Dra. Denisse Pastén
Group: Solar and Space Physics Group (GFS)
